My Hobbies

Skiing

I love going up and down the slopes here in Colorado. Early morning drives to the mountain with my friends just to spend the whole day skiing without a care in the world. It is easily my favourite part of living here.

Music

There hasn't been a day in recent memory where I haven't been listening to some sort of music. It's the soundtrack to my morning, it's in the background to help me study, and I don't know if I could live without it. I love discovering new tracks and artists to add to my collection.

Movies

I'm a huge film buff (pun intended). I try to watch at least one new movie every week and my watchlist has no end in sight. Like music, I don't know if I could live without movies. There is nothing quite like watching that movie you've been excited to see for months on opening night with your closest friends. There are so many things that can and will happen in my life, but for two hours on the occasional Friday, the world shrinks down to the size of a cinema screen, a large popcorn, and a cold Dr. Pepper.
