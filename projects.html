The Game Of Life

Designed and implemented a text-based, two-player strategy board game in C++ inspired by The Lion King. Players choose unique characters, paths, and advisors while navigating a randomized two-lane board filled with events, challenges, and strategic decisions. The game uses object-oriented design, file I/O, randomization, and menu-driven gameplay to manage player stats, events, and win conditions, emphasizing both software design and game balance.
